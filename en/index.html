Hello! I am Jaeheung Lee, a Process Engineering expert in secondary battery assembly, pioneering Smart Factory DX/AX.

With over 9 years and 8 months of experience, I have developed and set up pilot and mass production lines for 2170/4695 cylindrical and NCM prismatic batteries. My expertise covers various assembly methods, including laser, ultrasonic, and resistance welding.

At companies like Keumyang, JASTECH, and Eurocell, I led FAT/SAT inspections for dozens of assembly lines and successfully maximized production yield (from 40% to 98.7%).

Recently, to overcome traditional manufacturing limitations, I completed the Intel DX Master Class at Busan HRD Center. By mastering Python, PLC, and AI modeling, I am advancing as a data-driven intelligent manufacturing system engineer.
